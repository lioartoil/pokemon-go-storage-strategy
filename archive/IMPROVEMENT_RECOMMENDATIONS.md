## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### Concern: "What if I transfer a Pokemon I need later?"

**Mitigation Strategies**:

1. **Document Before Deleting**:

   - Take screenshots of Pokemon before mass transfer
   - Export data to spreadsheet if using apps like CalcyIV/PokeGenie

2. **Transfer in Waves**:

   - Phase 1: Transfer obvious excesses (rank 150-205)
   - Phase 2: Transfer middle tier (rank 100-149)
   - Phase 3: Transfer final tier (rank 76-99)
   - Monitor meta shifts between phases

3. **Meta Shift Recovery**:

   - New meta Pokemon spawn more during events
   - Trade with friends for better IVs
   - Re-catch during spotlight hours

4. **Reversibility**:
   - Lucky trades can recover lost species
   - Raids bring back legendaries
   - Events re-feature meta-relevant Pokemon
