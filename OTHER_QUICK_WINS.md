## Collector-First Principle

**All categories in this document follow the Collector-First approach:**

### **NEVER Transfer (Sacred Categories)**
- **4★ Pokemon (100% IV)** - Rare collector trophies (1/4096 odds)
- **Shiny Pokemon** - Collector value
- **Shadow Pokemon** - Limited availability, raid value
- **Legendary/Mythical** - Rare, expensive to obtain
- **Costume Pokemon** - Event-exclusive (keep 1 per costume)
- **@special Pokemon** - Legacy moves, irreplaceable
- **Lucky Pokemon** - Keep 1 per species (trade memories)

### **Target for Transfer**
- 3★ and lower of low-meta species
- 2★ and lower duplicates
- Common spawns caught for candy
- Event Pokemon extras (non-shiny, non-costume)

## Priority 1: Transfer Queue (INSTANT - 76+ slots)

### **Overview**

**Difficulty**: ✅ Easy
**Time**: 5 minutes
**Requirements**: 2× Transfer Candy event (optional but recommended)
**Expected Savings**: 76+ slots

### **What This Is**

Pokemon you've already marked for transfer (likely tagged `home`, `transfer`, or similar). These are ready to go - just waiting for candy event.

### **Step-by-Step Process**

#### Step 1: Find Your Transfer Queue

**Search Query**: `home` (or whatever tag you use for transfers)

**Expected**: 76+ Pokemon (you mentioned you have this)

**Verify**: These should all be Pokemon you've already decided to transfer:
- Common spawns you caught for candy
- Event Pokemon you kept temporarily
- Duplicates you marked during previous cleanup
- Low IV Pokemon you were saving for candy event

---

#### Step 2: Wait for 2× Transfer Candy Event (Optional)

**Check in-game news** for next event with "2× Transfer Candy" bonus

**Typical frequency**: 1-2 times per month

**Upcoming events** (check for 2× candy):
- Community Day (usually has 3× catch candy, sometimes 2× transfer)
- Spotlight Hour (sometimes has 2× transfer candy)
- Seasonal events (varies)

**Should you wait?**
- **If event is <1 week away**: Wait (maximize candy)
- **If event is >1 week away**: Transfer now (you need space more than candy)
- **If you're at <3% free space**: Transfer NOW (don't wait)

**Candy difference**:
- Regular transfer: 1 candy per Pokemon = 76 candy total
- 2× event: 2 candy per Pokemon = 152 candy total
- **Difference: 76 candy** (worth ~$0.50 in value, not worth waiting if you need space)

---

#### Step 3: Mass Transfer

**Process**:

1. Search: `home` (or your transfer tag)
2. Tap first Pokemon
3. Tap checkmark (top-right) to enter selection mode
4. Tap first ~20 Pokemon (don't select all at once)
5. Scroll down, tap ☰ menu
6. Select "Transfer"
7. Confirm transfer
8. Repeat for next batch

**Why batches of 20?**
- Prevents accidental mass transfer of wrong Pokemon
- Gives you chance to spot-check each batch
- Easier to undo if you change your mind mid-transfer

**Time**: 5 minutes for 76 Pokemon (very fast)

---

#### Step 4: Remove Transfer Queue Tag

**After all transfers complete**:

1. The tag should now show 0 Pokemon
2. Go to tag management
3. Delete the `home` or transfer tag
4. Tag is now ready to be reused for future transfer queues

---

### **Expected Results**

- **76+ Pokemon transferred**
- **76+ slots freed** ✅
- **76-152 candy gained** (depending on event)
- **Time**: 5 minutes
- **Immediate breathing room**

### **Safety Notes**

⚠️ **Before transferring, verify**:
- You haven't accidentally tagged anything valuable with `home`
- These are truly Pokemon you want to transfer
- No shinies, shadows, legendaries mixed in

**Spot-check**: Swipe through 10-20 random Pokemon in the transfer queue to verify they're all safe to transfer.

## Priority 4: Lucky Duplicates (50-100 slots)

### **Overview**

**Difficulty**: ✅ Easy
**Time**: 30 minutes
**Expected Savings**: 50-100 slots

### **What This Is**

Lucky Pokemon you have multiples of. Lucky Pokemon are great (½ dust cost), but you don't need 3+ copies of the same species.

**Why you have duplicates**:
- Lucky Friend trades (traded multiple of same species)
- 2× Lucky odds events (random luckies from old Pokemon)
- Community Day lucky trades (bulk trading with friends)

### **Retention Rules**

| Pokemon Category | Keep | Transfer |
|------------------|------|----------|
| Lucky + Shiny | ALL | Never transfer |
| Lucky + 4★ | ALL | Never transfer |
| Lucky + @special | ALL | Never transfer |
| Lucky + Legendary | 2 per species | Extras |
| Lucky + Raid Attacker | Per RAID_ATTACKER_COUNTS.md | Excess |
| Lucky + PvP Rank ≤20 | 2 per species | Extras |
| Lucky + Regular | 1 per species | All duplicates |

---

### **Step-by-Step Process**

#### Step 1: Find Lucky Pokemon

**Search Query**:
```
lucky&!shiny&!@special&!legendary&!mythical
```

**What this finds**:
- Lucky Pokemon
- NOT shiny (keep all lucky shinies)
- NOT @special moves (keep all lucky specials)
- NOT legendary/mythical (handle separately)

**Expected Results**: 100-300 lucky Pokemon

---

#### Step 2: Sort by Pokemon Name

**Action**:
1. After search, tap sort button (top-right)
2. Select "Name" (alphabetical)
3. This groups all same species together

**What you'll see**:
- All Machamp together
- All Eevee together
- All Community Day Pokemon together (e.g., Turtwig, Grotle, Torterra)

---

#### Step 3: Identify Duplicates

**Look for**: Same species appearing 2+ times in a row

**For each duplicate species**:

1. **Count total copies**: How many lucky copies of this species?
2. **Check PvP ranks**: Scan with PokeGenie (if not already scanned)
   - Any Rank ≤20 in ANY league? → Keep up to 2
   - All Rank 21+? → Keep only 1
3. **Check raid attacker status**: Tagged `#Attackers`?
   - YES → Check count vs `RAID_ATTACKER_COUNTS.md` recommendation
   - NO → Apply PvP rules above

---

#### Step 4: Choose Best Copy to Keep

**Decision criteria** (in order of priority):

1. **Best IVs**: 15/15/15 > 15/15/14 > 15/14/15, etc.
2. **Best moveset**: @special move > good raid moves > random moves
3. **Lowest CP**: Easier to max out later (saves dust despite lucky discount)
4. **Trade date**: Earliest lucky trade (sentimental value)

**Example**:
- You have 3× Lucky Machamp
- IVs: 15/15/15, 15/14/15, 14/15/15
- Moves: Counter/Dynamic Punch on all 3
- CPs: 2847, 2234, 1987
- **Keep**: 15/15/15 with CP 1987 (perfect IVs + lowest CP)
- **Transfer**: Other 2

---

#### Step 5: Tag and Transfer

**Process**:

1. For each duplicate species, tag the KEEP copy with `LuckyKeep` (temporary)
2. Tag the TRANSFER copies with `LuckyTransfer` (temporary)
3. After reviewing all lucky Pokemon, do final transfer:
   - Search: `#LuckyTransfer`
   - Verify all are safe to transfer
   - Transfer in batches of 20
4. Remove temporary tags

**Time**: 30 minutes for 50-100 duplicates

---

### **Common Lucky Duplicates**

**Community Day Pokemon** (you likely have multiples):
- Turtwig, Grotle, Torterra (CD September 2021, CD Classic)
- Piplup, Prinplup, Empoleon (CD January 2022, CD Classic)
- Shinx, Luxio, Luxray (CD November 2021, CD Classic)

**Lucky Friend Trades** (common species):
- Machamp (meta for raids, likely traded multiple)

- Legendaries (Mewtwo, Rayquaza if lucky traded multiple)

**Event Lucky Pokemon**:
- Any Pokemon from 2× Lucky odds events
- Old Pokemon (2016-2017) traded during guaranteed lucky period

---

### **Expected Results**

- **50-100 lucky duplicates transferred**
- **50-100 slots freed** ✅
- **Time**: 30 minutes
- **Lucky Pokemon collection streamlined**

### **5a. Community Day Catches (Non-Shiny)**

#### **The Problem**

You catch 200-300 Pokemon during Community Day. You keep:
- All shinies (for trading, collection, etc.)
- High IV non-shinies (for evolving, PvP, etc.)
- Random catches (forgot to transfer after event)

**Result**: 50-150 non-shiny, low IV Community Day catches still in storage

---

#### **Retention Rules**

| Pokemon | Keep | Transfer |
|---------|------|----------|
| Shiny | ALL | Never |
| 4★ (100% IV) | ALL | Never |
| 3★ (96-98%) PvP Rank ≤20 | 2 copies | Extras |
| 3★ (96-98%) Rank 21+ | 1 copy | Extras |
| 2★ or lower | 0 | ALL |
| Evolved non-shiny | 1 final evo | All pre-evos |

---

#### **Step-by-Step Process**

**Step 1: Identify last 3-6 Community Days**

**Recent Community Days** (check your catches):
- Vulpix (December 2025? - adjust to actual dates)
- Piplup (November 2025? - adjust)
- Etc.

**For each Community Day species**:

1. **Search**: `(species)&!shiny&!4*&!3*`
   - Example: `turtwig&!shiny&!4*&!3*`
2. **Sort by IV**: Tap sort → "CP" or "Recent"
3. **Keep**: 0-1 copy (if sentimental or evolved with CD move)
4. **Transfer**: All others

**Time**: 10 minutes per Community Day species

---

**Step 2: Handle Evolved Forms**

**Search**: `(evolved species)&!shiny&!4*&!3*`
- Example: `torterra&!shiny&!4*&!3*`

**Decision**:
- If it has Community Day move (@special) → Keep 1
- If it's high level (30+) → Keep 1 (good for raids)

**Step 3: Clean Up Pre-Evolutions**

**Search**: `(baby species)&!shiny&!4*&!3*`
- Example: `turtwig&grotle&!shiny&!4*&!3*`

**Rule**: You don't need pre-evolutions if you already have final evolutions

**Transfer**: ALL pre-evolutions that are 2★ or lower

## Priority 9: Raid Attacker Excess (50-100 slots)

### **Overview**

**Difficulty**: ⚠️ Medium
**Time**: 1 hour
**Expected Savings**: 50-100 slots

### **What This Is**

You have more raid attackers than recommended in `RAID_ATTACKER_COUNTS.md`.

**Example**:
- Recommendation: Keep 6 Shadow Machamp
- You have: 9 Shadow Machamp
- Transfer: 3 excess

---

### **Step-by-Step Process**

#### Step 1: Review Recommended Counts

**Open**: `docs/reference/RAID_ATTACKER_COUNTS.md`

**Note the recommended counts** for each attacker you have.

---

#### Step 2: Search Your Raid Attackers

**Search**: `#Attackers`

**Expected**: 200-600 Pokemon (depends on your raid focus)

---

#### Step 3: Count Each Species

**Sort by Name** (alphabetically)

**For each species**:
1. Count how many you have
2. Check recommended count in RAID_ATTACKER_COUNTS.md
3. If you have excess → Tag with `RaidExcess`

**Example**:
- Shadow Machamp: You have 9, recommended 6 → Tag 3 for transfer
- Lucario: You have 12, recommended 8 → Tag 4 for transfer
- Mega Rayquaza: You have 8, recommended 12 → Keep all, maybe get more

---

#### Step 4: Choose Which to Keep

**Decision criteria** (when choosing which copies to keep):

1. **Best IVs**: 15/15/15 > 15/15/14, etc.
2. **Highest level**: Level 40+ > Level 30-35 (saves dust/candy)
3. **Best moveset**: Optimal moves > needs TM
4. **Shadow/Mega ready**: Shadow > Regular, Mega energy invested > Not

**Example**:
- You have 9 Shadow Machamp
- Keep 6 with: Best IVs, Level 40+, Counter/Dynamic Punch already
- Transfer 3 with: Lowest IVs, Level 25-30, wrong moves

---

#### Step 5: Transfer Excess

**Search**: `#RaidExcess`

**Verify**:
- These are truly excess (double-check counts)
- You're keeping the best copies
- You don't need extras for trading

**Transfer**: In batches of 20

**Expected**: 50-100 Pokemon transferred
