# Solo 3-Star Dynamax Falinks — Max Battle Guide

**Event**: Max Monday — Dynamax Falinks
**Dates**: March 16-22, 2026 (6-7 PM local time on March 16)
**Raid Tier**: 3-Star Max Battle
**Boss Type**: Fighting

---

## Max Battle Mechanics (Quick Reference)

- **Eligible Pokemon**: Only Dynamax/Gigantamax Pokemon (no Shadow, no Mega, no regular)
- **Team size**: 3 Pokemon per trainer
- **Battle timer**: Boss enrages ~6 minutes

### Battle Phases

| Phase | Mode | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **Charge** | Real-time (tap) | Spam Fast Attacks to fill Max Meter (0→100) |
| **Countdown** | 3-second window | Swap to your DPS Pokemon |
| **Dynamax** | Turn-based (3 turns) | Use Max Moves each turn |
| **Reset** | — | Meter resets, return to Charge phase |

### Meter Charging

Both Fast and Charged Attacks give **+1 meter per hit** regardless of damage dealt. Since Fast Attacks are 2-7x faster (0.5s vs 1-3.5s cooldown), **always spam Fast Attacks only** during the Charge phase. Energy bubbles give +10% meter — swipe them immediately.

## Falinks Type Matchups

**Falinks is Fighting-type** — weak to Psychic, Flying, Fairy (all 1.6x in GO).

| Pokemon | Types | Offense vs Falinks | Defense vs Fighting |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Gardevoir** | Psychic/Fairy | Double SE (both STABs) | **Double resist (0.39x)** |
| **Metagross** | Steel/Psychic | Psychic SE | Neutral (1.0x) — Steel WEAK + Psychic resist cancel |
| **Zacian (Crowned)** | Fairy/Steel | Fairy SE | Neutral (1.0x) — Fairy resist + Steel WEAK cancel |
| Alakazam | Psychic | Psychic SE | Resists (0.625x) |
| Espeon | Psychic | Psychic SE | Resists (0.625x) |

**Note**: Steel is WEAK to Fighting, not resistant. Metagross and Zacian take neutral damage (weakness + resistance cancel out).

## Solo Battle Flow

### Cycle 1: Charge

1. Lead with **Metagross** (charger/tank)
2. **Spam Fast Attacks only** — do NOT use Charged Attacks
3. Swipe **energy bubbles** when they appear
4. **Dodge boss Charged Attacks** — watch for 3 white lines above your Pokemon, swipe left/right
5. When meter hits 100% → 3-second countdown starts
6. During countdown → **swap to Gardevoir**

### Cycle 2: Dynamax Burst

7. Gardevoir Dynamaxes for **3 turns**
8. Use **Max Mindstorm** (Psychic) or **Max Starfall** (Fairy) each turn — both super effective
9. After 3 turns → reverts to normal, meter resets

### Cycle 3: Repeat

10. **Switch back to Metagross** → charge meter again
11. When meter fills → **swap to backup DPS**
12. Dynamax for another 3-turn burst
13. Repeat until Falinks is defeated

## Key Rules

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Spam Fast Attacks during charge phase | Use Charged Attacks (same meter gain but 2-7x slower) |
| Swipe energy bubbles immediately | Ignore bubbles floating on screen |
| Dodge boss Charged Attacks (swipe on 3 white lines) | Try to dodge Fast Attacks (undodgeable) |
| Swap to DPS during the 3-second countdown | Dynamax your charger (wastes damage window) |
| Exit and retry if going badly | Keep fighting a lost cause (no MP cost on failure) |
